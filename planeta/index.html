Get ready Explorers!

Although Roghan has a busy schedule of expanding PQ’s multiverse and creating suspenseful political conflict (no biggie right?), he's always happy to find time for another Story Corner! On May 20, 2022, PQ’s very own Lore Master, Roghan Duggan Metcalf, will go live on our Discord and answer all your burning questions.

He’ll explain how he lays the groundwork for the story, how to introduce characters and give you some exclusive tips and tricks. Whether you are an aspiring writer or just want to get a peak behind the scenes, this talk is something you surely don’t want to miss!
